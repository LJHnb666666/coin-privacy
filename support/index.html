常见问题

如何购买 Coins？
登录后，在个人资料页点击 Coins 卡片，选择套餐并完成 Apple 支付。

如何恢复购买？
在购买页面点击「恢复购买」。Coins 为消耗型商品，已使用的 Coins 无法恢复，但未完成的交易会自动同步。

如何删除账号？
个人资料 → 账号与安全 → 删除账号。此操作不可撤销。

FAQ

How do I buy Coins?
Sign in, tap the Coins card on Profile, choose a pack, and complete purchase via Apple.

How do I restore purchases?
Tap "Restore Purchases" on the purchase screen. Coins are consumable; used Coins cannot be restored, but unfinished transactions will sync.

How do I delete my account?
Profile → Account & Security → Delete Account. This cannot be undone.

Is AI advice professional?
No. AI output is for reference only, not professional advice. Consult qualified professionals for medical, legal, or financial matters.
