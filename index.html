Coin - Privacy Policy / 隐私政策

最后更新：2026年7月7日

1. 简介

Coin（以下简称「本应用」）是一款 AI 决策引导工具，帮助您梳理困惑并获取参考建议。我们重视您的隐私。本隐私政策说明我们如何收集、使用、存储和保护您的个人信息。

使用本应用即表示您同意本政策的条款。如您不同意，请停止使用本应用。

2. 我们收集的信息

  • 账号信息：通过 Apple 或 Google 登录时，我们可能获取您的用户标识、邮箱地址、显示名称及头像 URL。
  • 您输入的内容：您提交的问题、决策过程中的回答，以及 AI 生成的建议与总结。
  • 使用记录：决策历史（Thought Paths）、剩余 Coins 数量、购买记录。
  • 反馈信息：您通过「获取支持」提交的反馈内容，以及您自愿提供的邮箱或电话。
  • 设备与安全信息：通过 Firebase App Check 收集的设备 attestation 数据，用于防止滥用。
  • 语言偏好：您选择的应用显示语言。

3. 信息的使用方式

我们收集的信息用于：提供 AI 决策引导等核心功能；管理 Coins 余额与 App 内购买；登录后跨设备同步决策历史；响应反馈与技术支持；保障服务安全；改进产品体验。

4. AI 处理说明

本应用使用 Google Gemini 等 AI 服务处理您输入的问题与回答。AI 生成的内容仅供参考，不构成专业的心理咨询、医疗、法律或财务建议。请勿输入高度敏感的个人身份信息。您的输入内容会发送至我们的后端服务器，并转发至 AI 服务提供商进行处理。

5. 第三方服务

本应用使用 Firebase（Google）、Google Sign-In、Sign in with Apple、Google Gemini、Apple StoreKit 等第三方服务。各第三方均有独立的隐私政策。

6. 数据存储与安全

您的账号信息与决策历史存储在 Google Cloud Firestore 中。未登录用户的记录可能暂存于设备本地。我们采用 Firebase App Check、服务端身份验证等措施保护 API 访问。购买交易由 Apple 处理并通过服务端验证。

7. 数据共享

我们不会出售您的个人信息。仅在向 AI 服务提供商发送必要内容、使用基础设施提供商存储数据，或法律法规要求时可能共享数据。

8. 您的权利

您有权：

  • 访问和查看您的决策历史；
  • 删除单条 Thought Path 记录；
  • 在应用内退出登录；
  • 永久删除账号及服务器上的个人数据：打开 个人资料 → 账号与安全 → 删除账号，按提示确认即可。此操作不可撤销，将删除您的账号资料、决策历史、Coins 余额及关联购买记录。

如需其他数据相关请求，也可通过「获取支持」联系我们，或访问支持页面。

9. 儿童隐私

本应用不面向 13 周岁以下的儿童，不会故意收集儿童的个人信息。

10. 政策变更

我们可能不时更新本隐私政策。更新后的版本将在此页面发布，并更新「最后更新」日期。

11. 联系我们

如对本隐私政策有任何疑问，请通过应用内「个人资料 → 获取支持」功能联系我们，或访问支持页面。用户协议请参阅用户协议。

1. Introduction

Coin ("the App") is an AI-powered decision guidance tool that helps you clarify dilemmas and receive reference suggestions. We value your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information.

By using the App, you agree to this Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the App.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: When you sign in with Apple or Google, we may receive your user ID, email address, display name, and profile photo URL.
  • Content You Provide: Questions you submit, answers during the decision process, and AI-generated suggestions and summaries.
  • Usage Data: Decision history (Thought Paths), remaining Coins balance, and purchase records.
  • Feedback: Content submitted through "Get Support," plus optional email or phone number.
  • Device & Security Data: Device attestation data via Firebase App Check to prevent abuse.
  • Language Preference: Your selected app display language.

3. How We Use Information

We use collected information to provide AI decision guidance, manage Coins and purchases, sync history across devices, respond to support requests, maintain security, and improve the product.

4. AI Processing

The App uses Google Gemini and other AI services to process your input. AI-generated content is for reference only and does not constitute professional psychological, medical, legal, or financial advice. Please do not enter highly sensitive personal information. Your input is sent to our servers and forwarded to AI providers.

5. Third-Party Services

The App uses Firebase (Google), Google Sign-In, Sign in with Apple, Google Gemini, and Apple StoreKit. Each provider has its own privacy policy.

6. Data Storage & Security

Account information and decision history are stored in Google Cloud Firestore. Unsigned-in records may be stored locally. We use Firebase App Check and server-side authentication. Purchases are processed by Apple with server-side verification.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data only when sending input to AI providers, using infrastructure providers, or when required by law.

8. Your Rights

You may:

  • Access and view your decision history;
  • Delete individual Thought Path records;
  • Sign out in the App;
  • Permanently delete your account and server-side personal data via Profile → Account & Security → Delete Account and confirm. This is irreversible and deletes your profile, decision history, Coins balance, and linked purchase records.

For other data requests, contact us via "Get Support" or visit our Support page.

9. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions will be posted on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please use Profile → Get Support in the App or visit our Support page. See our Terms of Service.
